The Monarch Organization

Monarch is a secret scientific organization founded in the 1940s to investigate unexplained natural phenomena. Beneath its public image as a research institution, Monarch’s true purpose is to study and monitor ancient giant creatures known as Titans, such as Godzilla and Kong. The organization believes these beings are not monsters, but primordial forces vital to maintaining Earth’s natural balance.

Despite its scientific mission, Monarch operates in moral gray areas. It conceals information from the public, conducts high-risk experiments, and makes controversial decisions in the name of global stability. As both a guardian and a threat, Monarch represents humanity’s struggle to understand whether nature should be controlled or respected and coexisted with.
