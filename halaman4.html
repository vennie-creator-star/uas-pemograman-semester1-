Rodan: He Who Was Born from Fire

Rodan is a volcanic Titan whose biology is intrinsically linked to extreme heat and tectonic activity. Dormant within magma chambers for centuries, he emerges during periods of intense geological upheaval. Rodan functions as a living natural disaster, reshaping landscapes through sheer thermal force.

Primary Abilities:

  • Volcanic PhysiologyHis body temperature rivals molten lava, allowing him to survive and thrive within active volcanoes.
  • Supersonic Flight:Capable of flying at hypersonic speeds, generating shockwaves and hurricane-force winds that devastate cities below.
  • Magma Armor:His hardened, lava-like skin provides immense resistance to physical and thermal damage.
  • Aerial Shockwaves:Wingbeats produce explosive pressure waves capable of destroying structures and disorienting other Titans.
  • Alpha Submission Instinct:Though highly aggressive, Rodan instinctively submits to stronger alpha Titans such as King Ghidorah.
